My Favorite Organization and Fundraising Event

Awesome Socks Club is a subscription service in which subscribers receive socks monthly. The after-tax profits from subscriptions are donated to efforts to decrease maternal and child mortality in Sierra Leone. Awesome Socks Club has donated over $900,000 so far.

Games Done Quick is a speedrun marathon that holds speedrunning marathons throughout the year to raise money for various charities. The two main events are Awesome Games Done Quick, usually held in early January, and Summer Games Done Quick, which is typically held at some point between May and July. Both events last for seven days and are streamed to a live audience. Awesome Games Done Quick raises money for the Prevent Cancer Foundation, and Summer Games Done Quick raises money for Doctors Without Borders. In total, GDQ has raised over $46,000,000 for charity. Personally, I've watched GDQ's events since January of 2019 when I was introduced to AGDQ by my older brother.
